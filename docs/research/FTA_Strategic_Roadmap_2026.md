# FTA Strategic Roadmap 2026 (Revised)

## Overview
Based on expert engineering feedback, the FTA (Field Transistor Alternative) project will prioritize high-value niche markets where its unique physical properties (high temperature, radiation hardness, 3D monolithic integration) provide an immediate advantage over traditional Silicon.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Completed)
- ✅ Physics simulation framework (`FTASolver`)
- ✅ Basic logic gate designs (NOT, D-Latch)
- ✅ Verification of 198x Gain at 400K

## Phase 2: Engineering Enhancements (Q2 2026)
- ✅ **Ferroelectric HZO Model:** Lowering V_DD to 1.0V using Negative Capacitance.
- ✅ **Differential Logic:** Solving linearity and CMRR issues for analog applications.
- ✅ **Verilog-A Model:** Standardized SPICE behavioral model for industry adoption.
- [ ] **ALD Calibration:** Modeling surface roughness for sub-3nm gap reliability.

## Phase 3: Niche Market Entry (Q3 2026 - Q1 2027)
- **Target 1: Space & Aviation**
    - Leverage radiation hardness (no P-N junctions) and 400K+ operation stability.
    - Focus: Satellite control logic, deep-space sensor interfaces.
- **Target 2: Industrial High-Voltage Control**
    - Utilize FTA's high breakdown voltage and inherent isolation.
    - Focus: Smart Grid switches, motor controllers, medical isolators.
- **Target 3: Precision Magnetic Sensing**
    - Exploiting the Cross-Field sensitivity for sub-mT resolution.
    - Focus: Quantum sensing, scientific instrumentation.

## Phase 4: Scaling & Integration (2027+)
- **BEOL (Back-End-of-Line) Integration:** 3D stacking of FTA layers over legacy CMOS.
- **MVL (Multi-Valued Logic):** Transitioning to Base-10 computing.
- **Cryo-FTA:** Direct-tunneling optimization for Quantum Computing 4K environments.

---

## Strategic Shift
"FTA is no longer just a transistor replacement; it is a high-performance cross-field computing platform for environments where Silicon fails."
